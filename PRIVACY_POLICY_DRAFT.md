# Privacy Policy for Bash MM

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Bash MM is operated for Bash M. Money Financial Services. This policy explains how the app handles information used to manage member savings, loans, transactions, and account records.

## Information We Collect

The app may collect and store:

- Member names
- Phone numbers
- National ID numbers
- Account numbers
- Profile photos
- Signature images
- Savings, withdrawal, loan disbursement, and loan repayment records
- Login credentials, passwords, and PINs in protected/hashed form
- Admin profile details

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:

- Create and manage member accounts
- Allow administrators and members to sign in
- Record savings, withdrawals, loans, and repayments
- Show account balances and transaction history
- Generate statements and financial reports
- Support internal financial record keeping

## Data Sharing

We do not sell member personal information. Data is shared only where needed to operate the app, maintain the backend service, comply with legal obligations, or support authorised Bash M. Money Financial Services operations.

## Data Storage And Security

The app sends data to the Bash MM backend API over HTTPS. Login passwords and member PINs are stored in protected hashed form. Access to admin functions is restricted to authorised administrators.

## User Control

Members who need account corrections, profile updates, or PIN resets should contact an authorised Bash MM administrator.
